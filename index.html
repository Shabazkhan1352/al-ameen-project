About Us

Our Educational Journey

Welcome to Al-Ameen Institute of Information Sciences (AIIS), where innovation meets education. As a proud initiative of the renowned Al-Ameen Educational Society, AIIS embodies a commitment to excellence in the field of information sciences. With cutting-edge Multi Lab facilities and a comprehensive suite of Corporate Training Courses, we empower our students to thrive in a rapidly evolving technological landscape. Our dedicated team of qualified teaching staff ensures that every student receives the guidance they need to succeed. Offering Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) and Master of Computer Science (M.Sc) programs, affiliated with Bangalore City University and recognized by key industrial and corporate sectors in Bangalore and Karnataka, AIIS is poised to shape the future of IT professionals. Join us as we embark on a journey of knowledge, growth, and innovation.
